+WEAPON.AMMO_OPTIONAL

Scale 2.0

{
Spawn:
RNFL F 1
loop

TOAH A 0 ACS_ExecuteAlways(998,0,44)

TNT1 AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAA 0 A_Lower
